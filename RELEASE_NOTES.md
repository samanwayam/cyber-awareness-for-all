**Tag:** `v1.0.0`

## 🚀 Initial Release — Cyber Awareness for Kids

This is the first stable release of **Cyber Awareness for All**, focused on children's online safety education.

---

## 📦 What's Included

### 📊 Cyber Safety Presentation (`presentations/`)
- 10-slide PowerPoint deck in a navy/teal theme
- Covers: Why cyber safety matters, 5 golden rules, online dangers, safe social media, password safety, parent & educator guide, and key takeaways
- Ready to use in classrooms, workshops, or awareness sessions

### ✅ Interactive Checklist (`checklists/`)
- 32 actionable items across 6 sections
- Sections: Personal Information, Passwords, Social Media, Threat Recognition, Home & Device Safety, Digital Citizenship
- Tagged by audience: Kids, Parents, or Both
- Live progress bar with completion tracking
- Fully printable

### 🎓 Awareness Module (`modules/`)
- 5-module interactive learning course
- Features: scenario-based learning, multiple-choice quizzes with instant feedback, clickable danger reveals, and a signable pledge
- Earns a personalized completion certificate
- Works fully offline — no login or data collection
